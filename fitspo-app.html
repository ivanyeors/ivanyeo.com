Fitspo App

A revolutionary fitness application that combines artificial intelligence with personalized workout routines. The app features an AI voice coach that guides users through exercises, provides real-time form corrections, and adapts workouts based on individual progress.

Key Features

  • AI-powered voice coaching for guided workouts
  • Computer vision technology for form correction
  • Personalized workout routines based on fitness level
  • Injury prevention through movement analysis
  • Progress tracking and performance metrics

Problem Statement

Many people struggle to maintain proper form during workouts, leading to reduced effectiveness and potential injuries. Traditional fitness apps lack real-time feedback and personalization, making it difficult for users to achieve their fitness goals safely and efficiently.

Proposed Solution

Fitspo App combines cutting-edge AI technology with exercise science to create a personal trainer experience that's accessible anytime, anywhere. The app uses your device's camera to analyze movement patterns, while the AI voice coach provides real-time guidance and motivation.
