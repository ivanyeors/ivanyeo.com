Key Results and learnings

Designing GNC App was an interesting experience as this was my first project upon joining the company. I had the experience of learning while working on the project.

Created Design system to optimise design time

  • As the project was new, and to reduce as much design debt as possible, A design system is required to complete the project under tight timelines.
  • The design system helped the team to work on 200-300plus screens concurrently and was successfully delivered within the timeline

Working with multiple departments

  • Improve pipeline across multiple departments to improve consistency of the designs.
  • Optimise processes of onboarding departments to the design system and bridge design and development constrains

Client Collaboration

  • Experienced working closely with client, and proposed ideas, collaborated and worked together on the designs.
  • Listening to their intent might sometimes be the best solution catalyst
