Project Retrospective

As a startup, wearing multiple hats is part of the grind. I embraced opportunity to take on roles below.

Leadership & Resource Planning

  • Planned redesign approach, considering timeline, resources, and budget constraints.
  • Managed timeline and resources through effective delegation and workload pacing.
  • Nurtured junior designers by sharing technical and design knowledge.

Stakeholder Management Takeaways

  • Broke down redesign into clear phases and steps for successful completion.
  • Shared regular progress updates to keep stakeholders aligned.
  • Managed requests and requirements using prioritization frameworks to maintain focus.

Working with multiple departments

  • Improved cross-departmental collaboration for design consistency.
  • Optimized onboarding processes for departments to the design system.
  • Bridged design and development constraints to streamline workflows.
