Transforming a National Health App

BruHealth will evolve from a pandemic-focused app into a comprehensive health management platform. The redesign will introduce features for tracking overall well-being, including fitness, mental health, chronic conditions, and access to healthcare services. With a user-centered approach, the app offers personalized health insights, integration with healthcare providers and fitness devices, empowering users to manage their health more effectively and holistically.
