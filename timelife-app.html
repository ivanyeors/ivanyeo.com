TimeLife App

A comprehensive life management application that helps users track their time, set and achieve goals, and build lasting habits. The app combines time management principles with habit formation psychology to create a powerful personal development tool.

Key Features

  • Smart time tracking with activity categorization
  • Goal setting and progress visualization
  • Habit formation with streak tracking
  • Life balance analytics and insights
  • Customizable reminders and notifications

Problem Statement

Many people struggle to balance their time effectively and maintain consistent habits. Traditional time management apps often focus solely on scheduling, while habit trackers work in isolation, creating a disconnected experience in personal development.

Proposed Solution

TimeLife App integrates time management, goal tracking, and habit formation into a single, cohesive platform. By connecting these elements, users can see how their daily habits and time usage align with their long-term goals, making it easier to make meaningful life changes.
